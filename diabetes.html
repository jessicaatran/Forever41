Diabetes

  • Lifestyle
  • Food
  • Lose extra weight.
  • Check your blood sugar level at least twice a day.
  • Get A1c blood tests
  • Track your carbohydrates.
  • Control your blood pressure, cholesterol, and triglyceride levels.
  • Keep moving.
  • Catch some ZZZs.
  • Manage stress.
    • See your doctor.
Eat more
  • Healthy fats from nuts, oils, flax seeds, or avocados
  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • High-fiber cereals and breads made from whole grains
  • Fish and shellfish, organic chicken or turkey
  • High-quality protein such as eggs, beans, low-fat dairy, and unsweetened yogurt
Eat less
  • Trans fats
  • Packaged and fast foods, especiall those high in sugar
  • White bread, sugary cereals, refined pastas or rice
  • Processed meat and red meat
  • Low-fat products that have replaced fat with added sugar, such as fat-free yogurt
